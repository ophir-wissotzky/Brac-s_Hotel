Hotel Experience

Welcome to a secluded sanctuary on the southern coast of Brač Island, Croatia—where untamed nature meets understated luxury. Surrounded by fragrant pine woodland and set above dramatic white-stone cliffs, Azure Bay Hotel opens onto a private beach of crystal-clear Adriatic waters.

Our boutique hotel is an elegant retreat designed meticulously for comfort, privacy, and an uninterrupted connection to nature. Each guest room and panoramic suite seamlessly blends contemporary coastal style with authentic, locally sourced island materials. In-room amenities feature high-speed Wi-Fi, fully automated climate environments, custom-carved limestone wet rooms, walk-in rainfall showers, stone soaking tubs, and organic custom-scented bath selections. Expansive floor-to-ceiling windows and large private glass balconies frame unforgettable horizons, offering serene, sweeping sea views or peaceful vistas across the emerald woodland canopy.

As we look forward to our grand opening in 2027, every space is being shaped to perfection. This upcoming launch marks the arrival of a new benchmark for quiet luxury in the region, fusing world-class architectural design with the slow, poetic pace of the Adriatic. Standard check-in is scheduled for 3:00 PM, and check-out at 12:00 PM, supported by a 24/7 dedicated arrival concierge desk and elite early-access relaxation lounges. Ahead of the official opening, exclusive priority booking windows and preview registration tiers will be introduced for charter guests seeking to secure the very first seasonal stays, tailored services, and custom island itinerary options.

Architectural & Ecological Heritage

The visual and structural identity of Azure Bay is an intentional tribute to the island's geologic legacy. Hand-carved limestone from local quarries forms the bedrock of the resort's foundations, grounding the contemporary geometric buildings directly into the cliff faces. The architecture utilizes low-profile, terraced footprints that follow the natural topography, minimizing vertical impact and ensuring the property remains visually integrated within the pine canopy. Fully committed to green-certified infrastructure, the property features zero-emission target heating, structural thermodynamic airflow layouts, intelligent wastewater recycling systems, and extensive living green roof installations designed to host indigenous cliffside flora.

Retreat Amenities

Leisure, Wellness & Transit Complex

Set between forest and sea, our leisure complex is an architectural marvel designed to honor both profound relaxation and spirited adventure. The centerpiece outdoor swimming pool features an infinity edge that creates a seamless optical line directly across the Adriatic horizon, dissolving the boundary between the resort and the open ocean. Deep within the stone foundations sits our 1,500-square-meter Subterranean Hydrotherapy Spa, showcasing ancient mineral-salt pools, panoramic Finnish saunas, eucalyptus steam caverns, and tailored treatment packages utilizing locally harvested Mediterranean muds and lavender oil. For seamless arrival logistics, the resort coordinates private luxury speedboat transfers directly from Split Airport to our private deep-water mooring pier, reducing travel time to a scenic 45-minute crossing.

Restaurant & Gastronomy

Our signature culinary program celebrates the deep, time-honored traditions of the Dalmatian coast, completely reimagined through a progressive, world-class modern lens. Our fundamental philosophy centers on strict hyper-local sourcing and hyper-seasonal flexibility: daily maritime catches are delivered directly to our docks by generational island fishermen from Bol and Milna, wild rosemary and sea fennel are hand-harvested from our own cliffside gardens, and single-variety, ultra-premium cold-pressed olive oils are supplied exclusively by ancient nearby family orchards.

The Open-Fire Hearth & Heritage Methods: The beating heart of our kitchen is an open-concept stone hearth fueled by aromatic olive and oak hardwoods. Here, chefs utilize traditional coastal techniques, including the legendary slow-braised Peka method—where fresh local ingredients and root vegetables are slow-baked under a heavy, bell-shaped iron dome covered entirely in glowing embers for hours to lock in unmatched, meltingly tender depth of flavor.

Curated Vineyards & Sommelier Pairings: No meal at Azure Bay is complete without an immersion into Croatia’s rare wine culture. Our deep stone cellar houses an extensive collection of elite regional vintages. Guests are guided through customized tasting journeys featuring the island's signature bold, sun-baked red Plavac Mali grapes alongside crisp, minerally, salt-kissed whites such as Pošip, each curated to elevate and mirror the ocean breeze outside.

The Island of Brač

The hotel is situated along an extraordinary, untouched stretch of southern Brač, famed globally for its iconic white stone. Shielded by hills of dense pine, our location delivers an intimate microclimate with smooth maritime thermals.
